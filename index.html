More than a sport

Why learn tennis? ☀️

It's not just about the game. Picking up a racket gives you back a few things modern life keeps taking away.

💪

Exercise that doesn't feel like it

You'll forget you're working out. Tennis is a full-body workout disguised as the most fun part of your week.

🤝

The easiest way to meet people

New to Lisbon, or just want to widen your circle? A court is one of the friendliest places to make new friends.

🧠

An hour that clears your head

Phone down, sun out, fully present. Tennis is brilliant for switching off the noise and lifting your mood.

🎾

A sport you'll keep for life

Start now and play for decades. Tennis is one of the few sports you simply never age out of.

💚 And it's not just us saying it — research links racket sports to a longer life, a healthier heart, and a sharper mind. Some studies even associate them with up to ~9 extra years of life.

Sources: Harvard Health · PubMed study · Medium

How it works

Three steps to your first rally.

  1. 1

    Message me

    Tap the WhatsApp button and say hi. We'll figure out whether group or private suits you best. No commitment yet.

  2. 2

    Come to your first session

    Saturdays and Sundays in Almada, with class times slotted sometime between 4 and 8pm — we'll find the one that fits you. Bring water and curiosity, and borrow a racket if you need one.

  3. 3

    Actually play

    By the end of the summer you'll rally, serve, and keep score — and you'll have had fun doing it.

Hi, I'm Rasha

I'm learning to coach — and I haven't forgotten how it feels to start.

I'm training to coach through the Portuguese Tennis Federation — I've completed the theory, and I'm now finishing my official coaching internship. So this is the real thing, not a weekend hobby. And honestly, being early in my coaching journey might be the best part: I remember exactly what it's like to stand on a court not knowing what to do with your hands. I know the nerves. I know the self-consciousness. And I know how good it feels when that first ball finally lands where you wanted it.

That's the whole reason I started this — to give beginners the patient, no-judgment start I wish everyone got.

My favorite shot is a double-handed backhand — and I can fix yours. 🎾
